📖 Role & Term Glossary — Asian Languages → English
Mid Laner 中单 (Zhōng dān) / 미드 라이너
Player controlling the central lane. Requires high KDA and map pressure. Dominant position in LPL and LCK.
ADC / Marksman 射手 (Shèshǒu) / 원거리 딜러
Attack Damage Carry. Primary ranged DPS in bot lane. Win Rate is the most predictive metric for ADC performance.
Jungler 打野 (Dǎ yě) / 정글러
Neutral objectives specialist. Consistency Index reflects objective control over a season.
Support 辅助 (Fǔzhù) / 서포터
Vision and peel specialist. High Games_Analyzed indicates tournament regularity and professional experience.
Top Laner 上单 (Shàng dān) / 탑 라이너
Isolated solo laner. Valued for split-push and carry potential in Asian meta.
Challenger 挑战者 / 챌린저 / チャレンジャー
Highest ladder rank in all servers. Prerequisite tier for professional team tryouts in Korea and China.
KDA Ratio 击杀死亡助攻 / 킬뎃어시
(Kills + Assists) ÷ Deaths. Capped at 15 for normalisation. Score weight: 30%.
GameRadar Score 综合评分 / 종합 점수
Proprietary index [0–10]. Formula: (KDA×0.3)+(WinRate×0.4)+(Consistency×0.3). Source-agnostic and cross-region comparable.
LPL 英雄联盟职业联赛
League of Legends Pro League — China's top-tier LoL competition. Players sourced from Wanplus and PentaQ.
LCK 리그 오브 레전드 챔피언스 코리아
League of Legends Champions Korea. Data sourced from Dak.gg and OP.GG KR.
Consistency Index 稳定性指数 / 일관성 지수
Derived metric [0–100] based on consistency_score, games_analyzed, or tournament_participations. Score weight: 30%.
Smurf Account 小号 (Xiǎo hào) / 스머프
Secondary account used by professional players. Detected via abnormal KDA+WinRate outliers in ladder data.
